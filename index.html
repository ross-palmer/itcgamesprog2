Programming Year 2 SFML Labs 2022/23

This page outlines the practicals you will undertake this year. Practicals count for 20% of your overall mark as follows:

  • Eight lab exercises (8 x 1.5% = 12%)
  • Two lab projects (2 x 4% = 8%)

You will receive a participation mark for each practical and the results will be put on the grade centre on blackboard each week. You may need to put in some small additional time outside of the scheduled lab hours to complete the lab projects. Please note:

  • You must be present for the duration of the lab to get a participation mark, unless you have a legitimate reason for your absence (e.g. medical certificate).

  • It is your responsibility to ensure your work is demonstrated to me during lab time. If fail to do this, you will receive no credit for it.

  • The practicals should be completed by the deadline indicated for that lab. You may present work at the beginning of your next lab session, but beyond that it will be graded with a penalty. Any work presented that is two or more weeks late will receive no credit unless you have a legitimate reason as indicated above.

  • It is your responsibility to check your practical results regularly on blackboard and to notify me immediately if you have a query.

The source code and assets that you will need to complete these labs will kept under source code control on github. Read here first before continuing below.
